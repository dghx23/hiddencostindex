✓ KEY CONCEPTS MEASURED

1. Hidden Cost Total      – R/month spent on system failure
1. Shadow Tax Rate        – Hidden costs as % of gross income
1. True Disposable Income – Net minus hidden costs
1. Hidden Cost Index      – Hidden costs ÷ basic living costs

Example: Urban SA household
Net income:        R35,000
Hidden costs:      R11,800 (security, load shedding, medical, etc.)
Shadow tax rate:   31%
True disposable:   R23,200
